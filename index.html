About Me

My name is Victoria and I grew up in Orlando, FL. When I first discovered I could leave this city on my own, I was itching to explore. Traveling has become one of my passions and I try to do it as much as I can. I ended up moving to Los Angeles after seeking a different place I could call home.

Sadly, because of choosing to live in a very expensive city, I had to put my passion on hold. About a month ago, I decided to leave my second home and come back to Orlando to attempt to save money and pick up where I started. So far, I think this is a pretty good decision.
